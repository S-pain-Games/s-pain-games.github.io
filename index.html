Who are we

We are a group of university students who develop indie video games. We specialize in 2D resource management video games with a Pixel Art style. You can check out our projects in our Itch.io site.

Cookie Quest Maker

Create magical cookie minions and give them missions to complete. The Beta version is available right now!

Talk at daytime, Bake at night-time

Manage your bakery and talk to the townsfolk at daytime. Craft your cookie shaped minions at night and send them to help. Unlock advanced cookie recipes to bake different types of cookie minions.

Rebake the world

Tons of quests with multiple outcomes. Improve the prosperity of the town, make everyone happy.
